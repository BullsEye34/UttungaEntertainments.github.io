About

Our story

  • 2015-2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started our journey early with just a hope of educating and entertaining people through short movies

  • March 2017

    An Agency is Born

    We established our agency as Uttunga, which was later changed to Uttunga Entertainments

  • December 2017

    Transition to Full Service

    We came online, starting with Facebook and Instagram. We started selling out new services and slowly became professionals

  • June 2018

    Phase Two Expansion

    We have started working with big companies and studios to deliver them with good service, through designing logos, vector arts, editing their videos and photos, and creating websites.
